Best time to visit

This beautiful natural attraction is during the dry season in the Philippines, which spans from December to May.

Health Benefits

Believed to have therapeutic properties that can have a positive impact on our well-being.

Activities

Swimming: Take a refreshing dip in the pools of Malumpati Cold Spring, known for its crystal clear water.

Cultural

It is revered as a sacred place by the indigenous people of the area, who deeply believe in its healing powers.
